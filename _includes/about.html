Who We Are

The Niagara Palestine Coalition was founded in the wake of the October crisis in Palestine in 2023 and has members from diverse backgrounds including Palestinians, Jews, academics, union activists, students, youth, and retirees of different faiths across the Niagara region of Ontario. It has organized weekly demonstrations in Niagara on Sundays as well as fundraisers and educational events.

Whether you're a student looking educate yourself, a worker who wants to contribute your skills, or just a human being who can't sit idly by during a genocide, you are more than welcome to join us in our fight for a free Palestine!
